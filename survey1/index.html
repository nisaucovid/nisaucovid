NISAU Survey Form

The Covid-19 pandemic has considerably impacted the higher education sector in the UK and globally. The UK is a popular destination for Indian students, many of whom have been looking forward to commencing their studies in the UK from September onward but are now faced with confusion and uncertainty around whether they would be able to proceed as planned. We, the National Indian Students and Alumni Union UK are conducting this survey to ascertain the concerns and requirements of Indian students so that we can support universities and higher education stakeholders in responding better to Indian students' requirements.
